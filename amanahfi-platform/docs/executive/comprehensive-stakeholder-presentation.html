🕌 Comprehensive Islamic Finance Portfolio

Murabaha - Cost-Plus Financing
Asset-based financing with transparent profit disclosure. Perfect for vehicle, equipment, and real estate financing with full Sharia compliance and automated validation.
Musharakah - Partnership Financing
Equity-based profit/loss sharing partnerships for business expansion, real estate development, and investment projects with event-driven workflows.
Ijarah - Asset Leasing
Sharia-compliant leasing with optional ownership transfer, comprehensive asset lifecycle management, and automated rental calculations.
Salam - Commodity Financing
Forward sale financing for agricultural products and commodities with integrated delivery tracking and quality verification systems.
Istisna - Manufacturing Finance
Project-based financing for construction and manufacturing with milestone-based payments and comprehensive project management integration.
Qard Hassan - Benevolent Loans
Interest-free social welfare loans with social impact tracking, CSR reporting, and community benefit measurement capabilities.
